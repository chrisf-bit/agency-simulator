# Agency Simulator - Teaser Video Script
**Duration:** 45-60 seconds

---

## HOOK (5 seconds)

**[Fast cuts: stressed faces, client emails, budget spreadsheets]**

"What if your rising stars could learn to run an agency..."

---

## THE PROBLEM (10 seconds)

**[Slow motion: coffee spilling, phone ringing, deadline clock]**

"...without the sleepless nights?"

**[Pause. Screen goes black.]**

"Without the real clients. Without the real risk."

---

## THE SOLUTION (15 seconds)

**[Game interface appears - energetic music kicks in]**

"Introducing Agency Simulator."

**[Quick cuts: selecting pitches, hiring staff, checking stats]**

"Win clients. Manage capacity. Make the tough calls."

**[Show team collaboration, competitive leaderboard]**

"Compete. Learn. Grow."

---

## THE BENEFITS (15 seconds)

**[Split screen: game on one side, real agency life on the other]**

"Real decisions. Real pressure. Zero consequences."

**[Show happy team members collaborating]**

"Your future leaders — battle-tested before they hit the front line."

**[Quick stats flying in]**

"Strategic thinking. Commercial awareness. Client management."

**[Beat]**

"All in one session."

---

## CALL TO ACTION (10 seconds)

**[Logo appears: The Amber Group]**

"Agency Simulator. From The Amber Group."

**[Contact details / URL]**

"Ready to develop your talent the exciting way?"

---

## TOTAL: ~55 seconds

---

### PRODUCTION NOTES

**Tone:** Confident, modern, slightly edgy — speaks to L&D buyers and agency leaders

**Music:** Building electronic/corporate track, energetic but professional

**Visuals:** Mix of cinematic "agency stress" b-roll and crisp game UI captures

**Pace:** Fast cuts in the middle, slower and more impactful at start and end

**Key message:** Risk-free learning that's actually engaging

---

### OPTIONAL EXTENDED VERSION (+15 seconds)

**[Testimonial snippet]**

"We learned more in two hours than six months of shadowing."
— *Rising Star, Creative Agency*

**[Back to logo]**

"See it in action. Book a demo today."
