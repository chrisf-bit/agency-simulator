### Q1: Stabilize
- Don't change much
- Assess starting clients (who's at risk?)
- Invest moderately in satisfaction
- Pitch for 1-2 good opportunities
- **Goal:** Understand the game, don't go bankrupt

### Q2-3: Build
- Invest in tech + training (compounds over time)
- Target existing client projects (easy wins)
- Keep utilization 70-85%
- Build reputation with premium quality on key clients
- **Goal:** Grow profitably, build reputation

### Q4-5: Optimize
- You should know what works now
- Double down on winning strategies
- Watch contract renewals carefully
- Start thinking about final score
- **Goal:** Maximize profit while maintaining stability

### Q6-8: Push
- Reputation and investments are maxed
- Can be more aggressive on pitches
- Protect cash buffer (don't go bankrupt at the end!)
- **Goal:** Maximize cumulative profit for final ranking
